The Pool

— How this portfolio works

This site is a swimming pool. Every object floating on the water is one project — drifting, bobbing, waiting to be picked up.

SCROLL — drift down the pool to find more works.

HOVER / TAP — an object shows its name. Tap again to dive in.

MIC — let the water listen. Speak, and the ripples answer.

Designed & built by Jiwon Yang — tap my name in the middle of the pool to read more about me.
