A Pocket Ocean on Your Desk

Tidepool combines a physical clicker diorama with an AR view. Going a step beyond a clicker toy that simply offers calm, every click feeds the fish in a virtual ecosystem, and through AR you watch them grow and swim. A tiny ecosystem that lives half on your desk, half on the screen.
