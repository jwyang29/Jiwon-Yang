Voice Waveform as Object

Seen from the side, the form is the recorded waveform of the words "I love you." Multiple acrylic sheets are layered so that together they trace a single waveform.

From Sound to Material

When a visitor makes a "sending" gesture in front of an ultrasonic distance sensor, LED strips beneath the acrylic — mapped to the distance of the hand — light up in sequence, completing a motion that feels like the message being sent.
