[ .About ]

Design You Can Touch, Hear, and Play

Hi! I'm Jiwon Yang, a designer working across physical computing, interactive installation, and creative coding. I build things that respond — sand that draws, organs that conduct light, sculptures that hold a voice still.

My work starts from a simple belief: interfaces don't have to be glass rectangles. Whether it's a fidget puzzle that whispers cues through vibration or a lamp that breathes, I look for the moment when technology stops feeling like technology — and starts feeling like play.
