Light That Breathes

Cocoon focuses on how a person actively handles an object, and on the supple movement of light. The more you pull the strap, the more the origami expands — and that degree sets how long the light will last. While the lamp is on, the origami keeps expanding and contracting, breathing life into the space along with its glow.
