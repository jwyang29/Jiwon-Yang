NodeXR
XR Collaboration · AI Copilot · Team Research

An XR collaboration copilot that makes group discussion visible for student project teams. Teammates think through a problem together in an XR meeting room while an AI structures every utterance into claims, evidence, questions, rebuttals, and agreements. The structured conversation grows into a live node graph — rendered in both 2D and 3D inside the XR space — and an AI agent, working from the discussion memory, gives real-time feedback on logical gaps and participation imbalance.
