Fold in Real Life, Collect in Game

Foldit turns origami into a game mechanic: a player folds a paper item and scans it with the camera, and AI object detection recognizes the fold and adds it to their collection. Breaking away from systems that rely only on in-game currency, here the game progresses through the player's own act of creation.
